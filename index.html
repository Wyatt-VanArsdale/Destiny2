Destiny 2


Destiny 2 is a looter shooter game developed by Bungie. As the player, you are a 'Guardian', who is empowered with a mystical power called the light to fight off evil.

As a Guardian, you fight off various enemy factions, getting loot, armor, and weapons from doing so


The goal of this website is to provide beginner infomration about the game!
